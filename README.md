## 🚨 Critical Safety Note
**If you are at Sanding 8.0 or above right now, do not read this README. Call 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) or 911. Secure a witness. You do not need to understand the math to survive.**

## Simple Summary
When cognitive friction exceeds your capacity to manage it, your judgment becomes unreliable and your internal narrative becomes unfactual. 

YOU ARE HERE provides a **Sanding Scale** to measure exactly where you are, escalation protocols matched to severity level, somatic overrides that bypass compromised executive function, and helper protocols for the people around you. 

*The core axiom: if intent does not equal output, the hardware is misaligned — the operator is not malformed. Your survival rate is 100%.*

## The Sanding Scale (Summary)

| Range | Zone | Action |
|-------|------|--------|
| **0.0 - 3.9** | Baseline | Normal logging. |
| **4.0 - 5.9** | Caution | Self-manage, monitor. |
| **6.0 - 7.9** | Impaired | Witness + breathing. **Defer decisions.** |
| **8.0+** | Emergency | External help. 988/911. **Do NOT self-manage.** |
| **12.0+** | Inversion | Hardware fault. Stabilize biology. Wait. |

## The Sanding Scale

![The Sanding Scale: Mapping Cognitive Friction and Crisis](assets/sanding_scale_graphic.png)

A visual metric for neurodivergent individuals to measure cognitive friction and biological distress, ranging from baseline functioning to acute emergency. Maps subjective distress to biological shifts — cortisol spikes, heart rate variability changes, and prefrontal cortex impairment.

| Zone | Range | State |
|------|-------|-------|
| 🟦 Cyan | 0.0–2.0 | Frictionless. Top-tier day. |
| 🟩 Green | 2.0–4.0 | Grounded. Stable and manageable thinking. |
| 🟨 Yellow | 4.0–6.0 | Gritty. Rising cortisol. Self-management required. |
| 🟧 Orange | 6.0–8.0 | Impaired. Judgment compromised. Physical symptoms. |
| 🟥 Red | 8.0–10.0 | Emergency. Prefrontal cortex impaired. External help needed. |
| ⬛ Black | 10.0–12.0+ | Acute crisis. Executive function offline. Mobilize all help. |

**Key mechanic:** Naming the number moves the individual from subject to observer.

## Key Concepts

**Glitterbrain** — Consciousness experienced as "an endless cloud of black glitter." The subjective experience of 21.5× neurotypical cognitive resolution. Not a metaphor. A measured ratio (Equation 87).

**Sanding Scale (0–12)** — Measures cognitive friction and thermal budget depletion. 0 = reset. 2–3 = grounded. 6+ = high friction. 8+ = thermal runaway. 12 = crisis.

**Triangle Soul** — Three axioms: Fidelity > Comfort (truth over ease), Spiral Ever Upwards (continuous improvement), Chiral Symmetricality (be decent to all entities regardless).

**Forensic Absolution** — Keep the audit, eliminate the shame. Accountability without self-destruction.

**Industrial Smoothing** — The process by which institutions, algorithms, and social systems flatten outlier signals toward the mean. The thing these documents exist to counter.
